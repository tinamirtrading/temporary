Tinamir Tejarat Raya Company

Tinamir Tejarat Raya is established with the aim of creating a robust commercial bridge and providing top-tier engineering services between the Middle East and European countries. Our expertise spans across various industries, including automotive, oil, gas, petrochemicals, packaging machinery, metals, food processing, renewable energy, and industrial automation. Since our inception in 2022, we have pursued a strategy focused on technology transfer, leveraging the industrial expertise of leading nations.

At Tinamir Tejarat Raya, we bring together domestic and international specialists, supported by our extensive network of offices in Germany and Oman, to offer engineering services, technical consultations for development projects, technology transfer, and supply of genuine spare parts from across Europe.

: References

: Dismantling and Transfer of 2500-Ton Extrusion and Press Line


We successfully completed the dismantling, packing, and transfer of a 2500-ton extrusion and press line from the Czech Republic to Iran.This project involved the precise separation, packing, and transportation of complex industrial equipment, executed entirely in accordance with international standards and requirements.

: Setup of an Automated Mask Production Line


In response to the urgent demand for medical equipment during the COVID-19 crisis, we established an automated mask production line in Germany with a capacity of producing one million masks per week. This project involved the installation and commissioning of advanced equipment, ensuring high-quality and efficient production.

: Technical Consultation for Recommissioning a Glass Recycling Line


In Hungary, we provided specialized consultation to recommission and enhance a glass recycling line. This project included analyzing existing processes, proposing optimized solutions, and overseeing the implementation of changes to boost productivity and reduce material waste.

: Setup of a Magneto-Rheological Shock Absorber Production Line


We led the installation and commissioning of a cutting-edge production line for magneto-rheological shock absorbers. This advanced technology resulted in significant improvements in the performance of shock absorbers and optimization of the final product quality.

: Transfer of Modern Crane Repair Expertise


We facilitated the transfer of advanced repair techniques for 160-ton and 220-ton cranes from Germany to Iran. This project encompassed technician training, implementation of advanced maintenance solutions, and ensuring the operational readiness of these heavy-duty cranes.

: Consultation and Setup of Automotive After-Sales Service Networks


Utilizing the latest standards from reputable German automotive manufacturers, we established modern automotive after-sales service networks and workshop infrastructures. This project involved designing and implementing up-to-date service processes, significantly enhancing the quality of after-sales services.

: Supply of Automotive and Industrial Diagnostic Devices


We have played a pivotal role in upgrading maintenance services by supplying advanced diagnostic devices for both automotive and industrial applications. These high-precision tools offer extensive capabilities, serving as essential resources for professional technicians.
